About Us

We've been preparing amazing southern cuisines for 13 years!

Managed and owned by Penny, Shawn and their daughter Skylar for 13 years. North 85 Diner was previously known as Penny's Place,situated by the Friendly Florist. When we moved to the North side of Highway 85 our Diner changed it's name to North 85 Diner. The Diner has a lot of positive reviews that you can read in our testimonal section.We try our hardest to provide an enjoyable place for you,your family and friends to come and enjoy our home made southern cooking!
